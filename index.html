About me

I believe that this opportunity would provide me with the ideal environment to develop my skills and gain practical experience. However, my plan B is to go to a suitable college which offers a course that will take me into the working environment in IT and Network administration.

I have an in-depth knowledge of both computer hardware and software, as well as good knowledge and understanding of various IT systems. In addition to my technical skills, I possess personal skills and attributes that are essential in the IT and Network Administration industry.
